Delivery

SHIPPING
POLICY

📦
Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment is confirmed. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order ships.

🚚
Delivery Time

Delivery takes between 5–14 business days depending on your location and carrier. We do our best to get it to you as fast as possible.

💸
Shipping Rates

Free shipping on orders over $20. Orders under $20 ship for a flat rate of $5.99. No hidden fees.

United States5–10 business days Free over $20
Canada7–14 business days

We currently ship to the United States and Canada only. Once your order ships you'll receive tracking info by email. If you haven't received it within the estimated window, contact us and we'll look into it right away.

Returns & Refunds

RETURNS &
REFUNDS

↩️
7-Day Return Window

You have 7 days from delivery to request a return. We accept returns only for items that arrived damaged, defective, or were the wrong item — not for change of mind.

📸
How to Request

Email us at scoopxus@gmail.com with your name, the email used at checkout, a photo of the issue, and a brief description. We'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.

💳
Refunds

Once your return is approved, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the error was ours.

Return Window7 days from confirmed delivery
Accepted ReasonsDamaged in shipping, defective, wrong item sent
Not AcceptedChange of mind, buyer's remorse, misuse
Refund Timeline5–7 business days after approval
How to StartEmail scoopxus@gmail.com with proof

We stand behind our products. If something arrived broken or wrong — that's on us and we'll make it right. For any issues, reach out and include a photo so we can resolve it quickly.
